In the future, man has invented a time machine to travel back in time. An organization called "The epoch council" has been formed to rule over time travels.
At the council's headquarters, the machine is kept and guarded 24 hours a day by the council's guards.

The Council has created a time travel directive called "The epoch directive" or the "directive". This directive determines how time travels should take place.
Changing events in the past can have huge consequences in the future and should be done with great care or not at all.
The council has investigated that a change in the past contributes to the change occurring in the same world and not in a parallel world.
With the knowledge to travel back in time, has given man a curiosity to verify history. The Council regularly conducts scientific expeditions to accurately document events of the past.

Curiosity is not appreciated by several religions that try to prevent people from using the machine. Religions do not want man to travel back in time to check the claims of religions and their truth. In several attempts, the religions have tried to sabotage the machine. All sabotage attempts have been stopped or avoided by the council's guards.

Recently, "evil" has succeeded in violently overthrowing the council and taking control of the machine in the council's headquarters. "evil" is a mad businessman sent by one of those religions. Now this religion has gained control of the machine through "evil" and no more time travel is allowed.

The Council has dispatched spies to its former headquarters and infiltrated the building enough to make the following known:
* Several of the time machine's energy cells are gone without a trace.
* There must be a secret list indicating where the missing energy cells are.
* "evil" has gotten hold of one of the machine's few control units and has disappeared without a trace.
* It is rumored that "evil" himself wants to change history to his own advantage.

The Council also anticipates that the religion, in control of the machine, will use it to change history to their own advantage without thinking of possible consequences. Other religions have warned that they will take up arms if they do not get access to the machine.
Now the world risks a world war because of a time machine that can reveal possible religious untruths. Experts have warned that a world war could be the end of the world.

The council has decided that the machine and its documentation must be destroyed. The documentation is securely locked away in the headquarters archive and will be destroyed when the council can reclaim the headquarters. Destroying the machine requires all energy cells to be retrieved and reassembled into the machine.

Now one person must infiltrate the headquarters and take back control to prevent a world war.
